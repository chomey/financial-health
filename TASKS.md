- [x] Task 100: Include investment return taxes in Estimated Tax with correct CA/US rules — Investment returns from taxable accounts generate tax obligations that should be reflected in the Estimated Tax card and explainer. The rules differ by country and account type. **Canada rules**: (A) Interest income (savings, GIC, HISA): 100% included in taxable income annually at marginal rate, even if not withdrawn. (B) Canadian dividends from public corps: eligible for dividend tax credit — gross up by 38%, then apply ~15% federal credit + provincial credit. For simplicity, model as taxed at ~25-33% effective (lower than marginal). (C) Capital gains: only taxed on realization — 50% inclusion rate on first $250k, 66.67% above. Not annual. (D) Tax-sheltered (TFSA, FHSA, RESP): no tax. (E) Tax-deferred (RRSP, LIRA): no annual tax, full withdrawal taxed as income. **US rules**: (A) Interest income (savings, HYSA, CDs, money market): taxed annually as ordinary income at marginal federal rate + state rate. (B) Qualified dividends: taxed at long-term capital gains rates (0%/15%/20%), not ordinary income rates. (C) Capital gains: short-term (<1yr) taxed as ordinary income; long-term (>=1yr) at preferential rates 0%/15%/20%. Only on realization. (D) Tax-sheltered (Roth IRA, Roth 401k, HSA): no tax. (E) Tax-deferred (401k, IRA, 529): no annual tax, full withdrawal taxed as income. **Implementation**: (1) In `computeTotals` in `financial-state.ts`, for each non-tax-sheltered, non-tax-deferred asset with `roiTaxTreatment === "income"`, compute annual interest as `balance * (roi/100)` and add to taxable income using `computeTax` with type `"employment"` (since interest is taxed as ordinary income in both countries). (2) For `roiTaxTreatment === "capital-gains"` on taxable accounts, do NOT add to annual tax — already modeled in withdrawal/runway. (3) In `buildTaxExplainerDetails`, include an `investmentIncomeTax` section with per-account breakdown. (4) In `TaxExplainerContent`, add a section below bracket tables explaining: which accounts generate annual tax, the country-specific rules, and a note: "Interest income is taxed annually. Capital gains and tax-deferred withdrawals are taxed only when realized." (5) Add unit tests verifying: CA interest taxed at marginal rate, US interest taxed at ordinary income rate, capital gains accounts excluded from annual tax, tax-free accounts excluded. Files: `src/lib/financial-state.ts`, `src/components/DataFlowArrows.tsx` (TaxExplainerContent), `tests/unit/financial-state.test.ts`. [@fullstack]
